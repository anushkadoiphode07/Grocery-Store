Our Story, Our 5 Channels, Vision and Mission

Samruddhi Organic has been fostering the culture of organic farming since 2004. We have over 75,000 acres of certified land and our 200+ products are now available in over 17 countries around the world. We follow Vedic as well as Biodynamic methods of farming wherein crops are grown with the help of bio-mechanism. We promote sustainability in agriculture by maintaining harmony between needs of farmers, customers and nature thus supporting the entire chain of healthy farming and living.

Our name itself means prosperity which we strive to achieve for all our stakeholders, through sustenance and conscious growth. We believe in Samruddhi for the farmer, the consumer and the planet. We believe in health & harmony, within and beyond!

The various activities of Samruddhi are being driven through its five important channels viz.

Food, Fertility, Fibre, Fodder and Fuel.

By following the process of 5Fs we have triggered the Samruddhi chain, a unique international network for the production and distribution of quality agricultural products subscribing to the strictest ecological and social standards.

Our

5 Channels

01. Food

Food vertical co-ordinates between participating farmers and end users to create a sustainable chain mechanism from farm to fork. This ultimately leads to ‘food-as it is meant to be’ giving the consumer a vast product range which are best-in-class and reasonable.

02. Fertility

Fertility vertical handles the responsibility of sourcing and managing the supply of the approved fertility inputs for crop nutrition, protection, Soil health and sustainable farming.

03. Fiber

Fibre vertical is responsible for co-ordination with participating farmer members to ensure a seamless flow of harvest from the farm to the place of processing.

04. Fodder

Fodder vertical streamlines activities related to certified fodder, which is a pre requisite for organically managed animal husbandry.

05. Fuel

Fuel vertical aligns the business activities to service the demand for fuel arising from the stakeholders.
